Young Scholars Initiative - Site Plan

Site Name

Young Scholars Initiative – This name reflects the organization’s mission to support and empower underprivileged students to complete their education through mentorship, resources, and financial aid.

Site Purpose

This website is designed to serve as a central hub for the Young Scholars Initiative, a charity organization that helps students from disadvantaged backgrounds complete their education. It will offer information on scholarship programs, mentorship opportunities, ways to donate or volunteer, and inspirational stories of scholars who have benefited from the program.

Scenarios

  • How can I apply for financial assistance or scholarships to help pay for my education?
  • How can I volunteer or donate to support students who are struggling to complete their education?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color (#2C3E50): Used for headers, footer, navigation bar, and buttons to convey trust and professionalism.
  • Secondary Color (#F39C12): Used for accents, icons, and call-to-action buttons to bring warmth and energy.

Typography

Font: Montserrat (Google Font)

  • Headings: Bold Montserrat for clarity and structure
  • Body Text: Regular Montserrat for clean readability
